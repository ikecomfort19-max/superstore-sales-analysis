# Superstore Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
SQL analysis of a retail superstore dataset containing 9,800+ orders.

## Business Questions Answered
1. What is the total revenue generated?
2. Which region generates the most sales?
3. Which product category is most popular?
4. Who are the top 5 customers by sales?
5. Which ship mode is used most often?
6. Are there orders where the company made a loss?
7. Which sub-categories perform above category average?
8. Who are the top 2 customers per region?
9. How does each state rank within its region?
10. What is the running total of sales per category?

## Tools Used
- MySQL Workbench
- Dataset: Superstore Sales Dataset (Kaggle)

## Key Findings
1. Total_revenue generated was 28609.939700000003
2. West region generates the highest sales
3.Office Supplies is the most popular category by orders
4. Brosina Hoffman was toping the list with total sales of $3714.304, Tracy Blumstein second followed by Gene Hale then Brendan Sweed and finally Steve Nguyen
5. According to data analysed standard mode ship mode is the most used ship mode
6. the company made a loss from 121 orders
7. various sub-categories performed more than their average category
8. Gene Hale was the top customer at the east
9. texas ranked no. 1 amongst all other state in sales.
